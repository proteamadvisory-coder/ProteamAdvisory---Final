Catalogue Activation

When tuned well, Flexera produces licence positions you can actually defend.

When deployed badly, the same platform produces application recognition gaps. ULM rules that drift quarter on quarter. Inventory connectors that silently stop syncing. A Technology Intelligence catalogue that everyone blames and no-one owns. And a Flexera subscription that gets harder to justify at every renewal.

The difference between the two outcomes is almost never the platform. It's connector strategy, normalisation discipline, ULM rule design, and operating model fit.

We've delivered or recovered 35+ Flexera deployments — Flexera One, FlexNet Manager, and migrations off Snow. We hold no reseller agreement with Flexera. Our only interest is your outcome.

Operational Flows

Six execution layers. One trusted catalogue.

Whether you're standing up Flexera One for the first time, migrating from FlexNet Manager or Snow, or recovering a deployment that's drifted — these are the workstreams we execute across every engagement.

— 01

Inventory & connector design

Configuring Flexera Beacon, FNMS Inventory, agent and agentless discovery, plus cloud (AWS, Azure, GCP), SaaS (SSO, expense, finance), and BYO connectors. Most failed Flexera deployments fail at the data layer, not the rules.

— 02

Technology Intelligence tuning

Technopedia is the catalogue, but tuning it for your estate is the project. Application recognition rules, custom Application Recognition Library entries, ARL overrides, version mapping, and the Flexera Data Platform settings that get most clients stuck at low maturity.

— 03

ULM rules & entitlement load

Loading your contract entitlement model — licence types, metrics, swap rights, downgrade rights, edition equivalence — into Flexera at a level of accuracy the engine can actually reconcile against. Use License Management rules tuned for Oracle, IBM, Microsoft, SAP, and Adobe.

— 04

Integration with ITSM & procurement

Connecting Flexera into ServiceNow, SAP Ariba, Coupa, and the broader procurement and ITSM estate — so contract data flows into entitlement, software requests check licence position, and approvals respect compliance constraints rather than working around them.

— 05

Reporting & governance design

Designing the dashboards, scheduled reports, and review cadences that turn Flexera into a governance system rather than a tool. Executive-level visibility for the CFO and CIO; operational-level reports for SAM, procurement, and security teams.

— 06

Recovery, remediation & migration

For stalled deployments, Snow consolidations, and FNM-to-Flexera-One migrations, we run a structured remediation. Diagnostic, prioritised fix plan, execution, hand-back. Most recoveries deliver trusted positions inside 8–14 weeks.

Platform Execution Model

A four-stage delivery. Outcome-anchored.

Engagements typically run 8–20 weeks depending on scope, with optional managed services to follow. Clear acceptance criteria at each stage.

01

Discover & scope

Free consultation. We agree the scope — net-new Flexera One stand-up, expansion to additional publishers, FNM-to-Flexera One migration, Snow consolidation, or remediation of a stalled deployment. Stakeholders, success metrics, and timeline agreed.

02

Diagnose & design

For new deployments, full discovery and design phase covering connector strategy and ULM rule architecture. For remediation, a structured diagnostic that identifies where the catalogue is producing wrong answers, why, and what the fix sequence looks like.

03

Implement & integrate

Execution. Connector tuning, Technology Intelligence rule design, ULM rule build, entitlement load, integration into ITSM and procurement, dashboard build. Delivered in two-week sprints with weekly progress reporting and clear acceptance criteria.

04

Stabilise & hand over

Knowledge transfer to your internal team, runbook documentation, and a structured 30/60/90-day hyper-care period. Optional managed services to sustain and continuously optimise the platform thereafter.

Normalisation Intelligence

What's running, what's licensed — finally aligned.

Technology Intelligence is the catalogue beneath every downstream answer Flexera produces. When raw inventory normalises cleanly into products, versions, and editions, the engine reconciles against real entitlements. When it doesn't, every report compounds the underlying recognition error.

Catalogue accuracy isn't a one-time tuning task. It's a discipline. We design the governance model and the operational routines that keep recognition rates high — not just at go-live, but at quarter two, quarter four, and quarter eight.
