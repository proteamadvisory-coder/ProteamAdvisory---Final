The problem we solve

Most enterprises overpay for software. Quietly. Year after year.

Software is one of the largest line items in the enterprise IT budget — and one of the least understood. Contracts are dense. Entitlements drift. Deployments outrun what you actually own. Renewal cycles bring more pressure than clarity. The result: spend that grows faster than usage.

Baselining is where we begin. We build a defensible licence position vendor by vendor, reconcile every entitlement against actual deployment and consumption, and translate that picture into action — harvesting unused licences, right-sizing tiers, restructuring renewals, and feeding the optimisation watch-list with new opportunities every month.

The managed service keeps the work compounding. AI models continuously surface anomalies. Automation handles the reconciliation. Our consultants act on the signals. Continuously baselined. Continuously optimised. Continuously audit-ready.

How we work

Six steps. Sequenced, not stacked.

Every engagement follows the same operating discipline — entitlement intelligence first, optimisation guidance last. AI and automation accelerate each stage while consultant oversight ensures complete accuracy.

01

Entitlement & Contract Intelligence
AI-Assisted

We analyse contracts, purchase records, product use rights, amendments, and vendor-specific licensing terms to establish a clear entitlement foundation. AI-assisted contract intelligence accelerates document interpretation — processing hundreds of pages of licensing documentation in hours, not weeks — while consultant oversight ensures complete licensing accuracy.

Includes
  • Contract & entitlement reconciliation
  • Purchase history analysis
  • Licensing rights interpretation
  • Vendor-specific metric analysis
  • Effective Licence Position support
02

Effective Licensing Position (ELP)
Automation-Led

We establish a defensible view of what your organisation owns versus what is actually being consumed. Automation reconciles deployments, usage, and entitlements at scale across thousands of devices and users simultaneously — dramatically reducing manual effort and delivering a position that is both faster and more accurate than traditional approaches.

Includes
  • ELP development
  • Deployment-to-entitlement reconciliation
  • Compliance risk identification
  • Over/under licensing detection
  • Vendor exposure assessment
03

License Cost Optimization
AI Pattern Recognition

We identify opportunities to reduce software spend without impacting business continuity. Using AI-driven pattern recognition and automation, we uncover hidden waste often missed through manual reviews — including dormant subscriptions, tier mismatches, and cross-vendor consolidation opportunities that traditional audits overlook.

Includes
  • Unused licence identification
  • Subscription optimization
  • Redundant application discovery
  • Tier & edition rationalization
  • Renewal planning support
04

Software Usage & Maintenance Analysis
Continuous Monitoring

Paying for software doesn't mean people are using it. We assess utilisation patterns, inactive users, maintenance costs, and adoption trends to ensure every software investment creates business value. Automated usage monitoring runs continuously — flagging deviations and new optimisation signals in real time between formal engagement cycles.

Includes
  • Usage analytics
  • Maintenance cost review
  • Dormant licence detection
  • Renewal optimization
  • Software consolidation opportunities
05

Hardware Usage Correlation
Automated Mapping

Software licensing rarely exists in isolation. We correlate infrastructure and hardware environments with licensing requirements to improve licensing accuracy and support optimisation opportunities. Automated asset discovery and infrastructure mapping feeds directly into the ELP — eliminating the manual effort of correlating hardware and software data across complex environments.

Includes
  • Server & infrastructure analysis
  • Hardware utilisation mapping
  • Virtualization impact assessment
  • Consumption-based licensing review
06

Contractual Optimization Guidance
Intelligence-Informed

Beyond technical analysis, we help organisations make commercially smarter software decisions. AI-informed benchmarking and market intelligence feeds into our strategic recommendations — so the guidance we give on renewals, negotiations, and vendor engagement is backed by data, not instinct alone.

Includes
  • Renewal strategy support
  • Commercial optimisation guidance
  • Vendor negotiation preparation
  • Future-state licensing recommendations
