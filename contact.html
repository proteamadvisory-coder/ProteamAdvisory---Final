Frequently asked

Questions we hear before the call.

A few common ones — answered briefly here, in much more depth on the call.

Is the free consultation actually free?

Yes — genuinely. There's no obligation, and we won't follow up endlessly if you decide not to engage. Our view is that a 30-minute conversation gives both sides enough information to know whether working together makes sense. Plenty of consultations end with us recommending you handle it internally.

How quickly can you respond if we have an audit notice?

Audit notices are time-sensitive — we treat them that way. Reach out by phone or WhatsApp for the fastest response. We can typically have a senior consultant on a call with you within 24 hours, often the same day for clients in time zones close to ours.

Do you take referral fees from software vendors?

No. We don't accept commissions, kickbacks, or referral fees from any software vendor. Our independence is the foundation of the work we do — and it's what allows clients to trust the advice we give. The only party we work for is the one paying our invoice.

Do we need to switch SAM tools to work with you?

Absolutely not. We work inside whatever SAM tooling you've already invested in — Flexera, ServiceNow, Snow, USU, Aspera, Eracent. We have no preferred vendor and no rip-and-replace agenda. Our goal is to make your existing stack work harder, not to add to your software bill.

What does a typical engagement cost?

It depends on scope, vendor coverage, and timeline. For most clients, we anchor commercial models to outcomes — measurable savings or reduced audit exposure — rather than time-and-materials. We'll give you a transparent estimate after the free consultation, once we understand what you're actually trying to achieve.

Can we engage you remotely or only on-site?

Either. The majority of our work is delivered remotely — secure data exchange, video sessions, and asynchronous reporting. For sensitive or politically complex engagements, we can also be on-site at your offices. We work in the way that suits your governance and security requirements best.
