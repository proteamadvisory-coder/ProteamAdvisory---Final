Walk → Run

Most enterprises buy FinOps tooling first and design the operating model second. The dashboards arrive faster than the discipline. Six months in, cost visibility is excellent — and cost behaviour hasn't changed. Engineers still don't own the bill. Finance still doesn't understand the engineering levers. The FinOps team still lacks a clear mandate.

The fix isn't more tooling. The fix is operating-model maturity — clarity on who owns what, what gets measured, how decisions get made, and how cost ownership flows through the organisation. The FinOps Foundation framework codifies this into a model: six capability domains, four personas, and three maturity phases.

Our maturity assessment scores you against the framework, benchmarks you against industry peers, identifies the 4–6 capability gaps with the highest leverage, and designs a sequenced 12–18 month transformation. The goal is the next phase — not a perfect operating model.

A four-stage assessment. Then a transformation, if you want one.

The assessment runs 4–6 weeks. The transformation roadmap is delivered with it. Execution support is optional and quoted separately.
