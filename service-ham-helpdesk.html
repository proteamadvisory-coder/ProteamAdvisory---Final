Within SLA

Hardware asset decisions tend to compound. A standardisation choice made in a hurry becomes a lifecycle commitment. A leasing decision made for the wrong reason becomes a five-year cost structure that's hard to unwind. A disposal that skips proper data-bearing-device protocols becomes a regulatory finding.

The problem isn't ignorance — most IT and procurement teams know what good looks like in the abstract. The problem is that hardware decisions are made constantly, often urgently, and rarely with specialist input. The cost of routinely asking a specialist before making each decision is high. The cost of not asking is invisible — until the audit, the disposal compliance review, or the next refresh cycle.

The hardware helpdesk gives you specialist input on tap. Senior HAM specialists, multi-category coverage, SLA-backed response, AI-augmented triage. The unit cost per decision is low enough that you can route the borderline questions to us, instead of letting them resolve by default.

What's included

One retainer. Every hardware lifecycle question.

Across end-user devices, data-centre infrastructure, and network hardware — from procurement specification through to end-of-life disposal. AI-assisted triage, senior specialist response, audit-defensible logs.

// 01
📋

Procurement standards advisory

Specification reviews before tendering or purchasing. Total-cost-of-ownership analysis, vendor neutrality checks, lifecycle planning embedded into spec — so the asset you buy in year one still makes sense in year five.

// 02
🔄

Refresh planning support

Refresh-cycle modelling, fleet age analysis, replacement sequencing, and capital budgeting input. The helpdesk produces the financial and operational scenarios; your team makes the call.

// 03
⚖️

Lease vs buy analysis

Vendor-neutral financial modelling for major hardware purchases. We model total cost over the asset lifecycle, accounting for residual value, refresh timing, and tax treatment — so the decision is made on numbers, not the lease vendor's pitch deck.

// 04
🏷

Tagging & reconciliation advisory

Asset tagging strategy, CMDB hygiene, reconciliation between procurement records, deployed inventory, and disposal records. The helpdesk gives you the playbook; your team executes.

// 05
🔐

Disposal compliance

End-of-life processes for data-bearing devices, secure data destruction, certificate-of-destruction tracking, and audit-defensible disposal records. Especially important under SOC 2, ISO 27001, and sector-specific regulatory frameworks.

// 06
📊

Quarterly intelligence report

Every quarter — query patterns, emerging vendor risks, refresh-cycle pressure points, and compliance issues spotted across your estate. Forward-looking, not just a log of what was asked. AI-generated trend surface, specialist-verified.
