How we engage

A four-step engagement model. No surprises.

Whether you hire us for one audit defence or sign a multi-year managed services contract, the engagement model stays consistent.

1

Discover

Free no-obligation consultation. We understand your environment, vendor exposure, current SAM maturity, and the specific outcomes you're aiming for.

2

Assess

Deep-dive analysis of contracts, entitlements, deployment data, and consumption reports. We surface the risks and opportunities that matter most.

3

Execute

We do the work — whether that's audit defence negotiation, contract restructuring, optimisation roadmap delivery, or ongoing managed services.

4

Sustain

We hand over (or stay engaged for) the ongoing operating model that keeps savings recurring and risk continuously managed.
