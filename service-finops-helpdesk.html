The problem we solve

FinOps is a discipline, not a tool. And it needs specialists.

FinOps tooling has matured rapidly. Most enterprises now have Apptio, AWS Cost Explorer, Azure Cost Management, or platform-native tools producing daily dashboards. The cost data is more visible than ever.

The decisions are not. Should we buy a 3-year compute Savings Plan now or wait? Why did Azure spend jump 22% last week — anomaly or growth? Should the FinTech unit be on BYOL or licence-included Oracle? These aren't questions a dashboard answers. They're questions a senior FinOps specialist answers, in writing, with the supporting analysis attached.

The cloud cost helpdesk gives you that specialist on demand. SLA-backed, platform-agnostic, decision-grade advisory. Most clients use it to support a small internal FinOps team that needs senior backup.

What's covered

One retainer. Every cloud cost question.

Seven advisory areas delivered under a single SLA — across AWS, Azure, and GCP, with the FinOps and licensing crossover most providers can't match.

01

Commitment guidance

RI, Savings Plans, Azure Reservations, GCP CUDs — sizing, term, renewal strategy, and break-even modelling with supporting numbers.

02

Anomaly investigation

When spend jumps and tooling can't say why — we isolate the cost driver and recommend corrective action, usually within 48 hours.

03

Tagging & allocation strategy

A tagging model practical enough for engineering to adopt and granular enough for finance to allocate. Showback and chargeback design included.

04

BYOL vs licence-included advisory

Vendor by vendor — Oracle, Microsoft, IBM, Red Hat, SAP — with the contractual reference attached. The single most common avoidable cost in cloud.

05

Vendor renewal support

Six to nine months ahead of EDP, EA, and Microsoft cloud renewals — we run the analysis and draft your negotiation position.

06

Quarterly trend report

Query themes, emerging vendor risks, and where the next optimisation opportunities are likely to surface — delivered every quarter.

07

Governance & policy advisory

Cloud spend policies, budget alert architecture, escalation thresholds — the governance layer that keeps engineering accountable without slowing it down.
