Why this team, in this category, right now.

SAM is having its decade. Software costs as a share of enterprise budget have doubled in five years. AI tooling has rewritten the contract landscape. Cloud is no longer a line item, it’s the largest one. The teams who can read this complexity and act on it are rare and getting rarer.

Proteam is the boutique alternative to the global generalists — smaller, sharper, and built around the principle that the people doing the work should be the people the client meets. If that resonates, you’ll fit here.
